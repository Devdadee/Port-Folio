Historically the desired to have text printed in Specific format meant that original manuscripts were "mark Up" with Annotation to indicate to the book printer how the author would like sections of the text laid out.this annotation has to be concise and needed to be easily understood both by the printer and the author. A series of commonly recognised abbrebiations therefore formed the basis of standard markup language. Hypertext Markup language(HTML) is a modern standard markup language that uses common abbreviations called "Tags" to indicate to the web browser how the author would like to have section of the webpage laid out.
